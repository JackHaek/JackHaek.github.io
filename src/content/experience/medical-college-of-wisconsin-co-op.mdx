Worked collaboratively to develop a python-based data pipeline
(Tensorflow, NumPy, and Pandas) to extract radiomic features from
spinal MRI scans to predict future pain and spinal degradation levels
for a patient. Iterations of the model were evaluated by experts and
iterated upon by implementing several bootstrapping techniques and
developing a method of continuous integration using a super compute cluster.
